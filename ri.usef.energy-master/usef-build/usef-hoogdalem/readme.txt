This project is a USEF RI based project for Stedin, implemented by BePowered BVBA. 

BePowered kindly shared their specific pluggable business components with the USEF Foundation (with permission from Stedin) to serve as an example for other USEF implementors.

The USEF Foundation facilitates distribution of third party pluggable business components for educational purposes.

The implementation is based on the USEF release of january 12th 2016 (1.2.0).
